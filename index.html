Welcome!

Hello, I'm Mrs. Vawter, the chief cook and beaker washer in the sixth grade science classroom. I get to inspire students to explore the world around them. This year we will explore the world through inquiry-based learning and the use of scientific processes. What we learn will help us:

    • ask better questions
    • create accurate models
    • analyze data carefully
    • identify fraud, and
    • communicate clearly.
It's going to be an exciting year with the new school and the new bridge in town. At the end of the year we will look back at how much we've grown through all our study...especially in science! :) Parents, please feel free to call, email, or stop by.

Grades...

are used to enhance student learning. They invite reflective, critical thinking. (At least that's the goal.) Sometimes a student will want to rethink their approach on an assignment. For example, if a student does not score well on a test, they can usually retake it. They must complete a study guide before the retake.

Required supplies for this class...

are a composition notebook, a pencil, and a gluestick.
