CHALLENGE

After completing your metric measuring wheel, measure a distance outside of the classroom. Write down what you measured and have a parent or guardian initial it. Some ideas for things to measure:

  • the length of your driveway
  • the distance from room 110 to the cafeteria
  • the distance from school to your house...if you live close
  • the distance from the new middle school to the old middle school
You can measure any distance; it does not have to be something on this list.
