CHALLENGE # 2

You have a choice this time! Choose ONE of the following and have someone verify that you did it.

  1. Demonstrate a chemical change using an empty water bottle, a balloon, 1/4 cup vinegar, and 1 teaspoon baking soda. Be prepared to explain how you know it was a chemical reaction.
  2. Calculate the density of an object by dividing its mass by its volume. (Some ideas: a rock, a lead fishing weight, oil...motor or olive or...?, pop).
