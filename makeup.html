Week One

Wednesday, August 19. Students interviewed a classmate and put the information in a Frayer Model. They measured a classmate's hand span using metric or U.S. Customary measurements. During this process, students learned classroom procedures and rules.

Thursday, August 20. Students watched a lab safety video, "Ultimate Lab Safety." The link to it is on the home page. After discussion, the class created their own list of lab safety rules. In groups or individually, students created something to explain lab safety rules. They could create a story, a poster, or a video.
